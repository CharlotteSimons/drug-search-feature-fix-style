Indication

Approved indication(s)

  • Adults with locally advanced or metastatic urothelial cancer (mUC), in combination with pembrolizumab.
  • Adults with locally advanced or mUC who have previously received a programmed death receptor-1 (PD-1) or programmed death-ligand 1 (PD-L1) inhibitor and platinum-containing chemotherapy, or are ineligible for cisplatin-containing chemotherapy and have previously received one or more prior lines of therapy.

Ongoing clinical trial indication(s)

Metastatic breast cancer (Phase 3)

Unapproved indication(s)

Genitourinary Cancer
